Brute is a note-taking app that allows you to create and organize your notes in a simple and efficient way. With Brute, you can easily create new notes, edit existing ones, and organize them into different categories. Whether you're a student, a professional, or just someone who likes to keep things organized, Brute is the perfect tool for you.

Brute is designed to be user-friendly and intuitive, with a clean and minimalist interface that makes it easy to navigate and use. You can create new notes with just a few clicks, and you can easily edit and format your notes to suit your needs. Brute also allows you to organize your notes into different categories, making it easy to find what you need when you need it.
